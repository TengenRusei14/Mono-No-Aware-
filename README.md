# Serious Samurai

A choice driven narrative game set in 1900 Japan.

---

## About

You were once the greatest samurai in Japan, a man known as “Serious Samurai.” Then your wife died. Desperate, you found a way to enter the land of the dead and bring her back.

---

## Story

You leave your home in Takanawa and travel farther than any living person should. At the end of the journey lies a frozen, empty continent where snow never stops falling.

The price of entry is your free will. In this place, you have no choices of your own. Every outcome is decided by the Wheel of Fate.

This is not the hell from old stories. There is no fire, no screaming only an endless quiet snowfield. Standing there, you begin to wonder: if the land of the dead is this peaceful, what would heaven even look like? And if this place is not a punishment, what are you really saving her from?

---

## The Wheel of Fate

You no longer get to decide your path. The Wheel spins at every critical moment, and its result is final. You cannot outwit it, you cannot outfight it. All you can do is face what it gives you and keep moving forward or choose to stop.

---

## Theme: *Mono no Aware*

At its heart, this story is about *Mono no Aware* the simple, sad beauty of knowing that everything ends, and that it matters because it ends.

---

## The Final Question

You came to save her. But standing in that silent snowfall, the truth becomes harder to ignore. Maybe she isn’t trapped. Maybe you are.

**Will you keep fighting the Wheel to bring her home, or will you find the strength to let her go?**
