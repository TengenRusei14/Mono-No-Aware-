# Mono no Aware

## Everything ends - and because it does, it matters more.

This is a Story set in 1900 Japan, Where Japans most greatest samurai "Serious Samurai" who has currently lost his wife
and sets of on a journey into what seems like hell in hopes of rescuing her soul and bringing her back to Takanawa 
neighbourhood where they may continue living together as a happy family.

But things take a turn when the land he had hopes of saving his wife from was a different place from the stories he was
told. No humans screaming due to torture, no fire all around him but instead an endless and desolate land that snowed 
every second

And he thought to himself "If Hell is this peaceful, I wonder what heaven would look like" and what would be the point 
of saving his wife from a land like that

## WILL YOU SAVE HER OR WILL YOU LET HER GO?
